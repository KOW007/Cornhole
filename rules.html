Corn Hole Rules

  • Boards should be approximately 27 ft. apart
  • Players must remain behind the "foul line" and inside the "pitcher's box"
  • There should be one teammate from each team on either side
  • Net/Cancellation Scoring: Each round the team that scores more points is the only team to win points, but must subtract the opposing team's points from their total
  • Any bag that rests anywhere on the board counts as 1 point
  • Any bag that makes it in the hole counts as 3 points
  • Play to 21
  • Win by 2
